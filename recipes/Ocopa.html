"Ocopa Recipe"

Description

A delicious green sauce served as a cold dish, usually given as the entry dish.

This dish is from the south of Peru and is served with lettuce, potatoes, hard boiled eggs and black olives

Ingredients

This is for 1 serving

  • Salt (One half of tea spoon)
  • Extra virgin Olive oil (7 spoons)
  • Fresh cheese (I love to combine different types like Pecorino cheese (Fiore Sardo)) 200gr
  • Penuts like 50gr
  • Huacatay 150gr
  • Onion (any Onion) 1/2
  • 3 Peruvian Yellow chilies
  • Sweet animal cookies (vanilla) 50 gr
  • Evaporated Milk 1/2 of a cup (I use cream milk)

Steps to make it

  1. Separate the huacatay leaves only
  2. Clean the leaves of any dirt
  3. Put the leaves in a pot with boiling water for 15 seconds and then drain all the water out of the leaves with a strainer
  4. Cut the onions in big pieces (or small)
  5. Clean the inside of the yellow chilies and then cut them the size of the cut onions
  6. fry the pieces of onions and yellow chilies together
  7. Once the onion is clear and the chillies are seared, put them in the blender
  8. Put in the blender the peanuts, the fresh cheese, the salt, the milk, the cookies and blend all the ingredients together
  9. served them with potatoes, a lettuce, half a hard boiled egg and as many black olives as you like
  10. Voila (there you have it...enjoy)
