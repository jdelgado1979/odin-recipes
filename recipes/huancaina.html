"huancaina Recipe"

Description

This is a very common sauce served as a cold dish, usually given as the first

A delicious yellow cream that is served with potatoes, eggs, lettuce and black olives

Ingredients

This is for 1 serving

  • Salt (One half of tea spoon)
  • Extra virgin Olive oil (7 spoons)
  • Fresh cheese 200gr
  • Penuts like 50gr
  • Onion (any Onion) 1/2
  • 3 Peruvian Yellow chilies
  • Soda Crackers 50 gr (half a package)
  • Evaporated Milk 1/2 of a cup (I use cream milk)

Steps to make it

  1. Cut the onions in big pieces (or small)
  2. Clean the inside of the yellow chilies and then cut them the size of the cut onions
  3. fry the pieces of onions and yellow chilies together
  4. Once the onion is clear and the chillies are seared, put them in the blender
  5. Put in the blender the fresh cheese, the salt, the milk, the crackers and blend all the ingredients together
  6. served them with potatoes, a lettuce, half a hard boiled egg and as many black olives as you like
  7. Voila (there you have it...enjoy)
