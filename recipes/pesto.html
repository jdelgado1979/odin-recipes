"Pesto Recipe"

Description

A delicious green cream mainly used with pasta, very simple to make, yet so delicious.

A true master combination of few ingredients that will make you want to crave for more

Ingredients

This is for 1 serving

  • Salt (One half of tea spoon)
  • Extra virgin Olive oil (7 spoons)
  • Fresh cheese (I love to combine different types like Pecorino cheese (Fiore Sardo)) 200gr
  • Parmigiano Reggiano cheese (Again we can use other ones like Grana Padano) 100gr
  • Pecans (the original is pine nuts) like 50gr
  • Basil 200gr
  • garlic 4 cloves
  • Milk 1/5 of a cup (I use cream milk)

Steps to make it

  1. Separate the basil leaves only
  2. Clean the leaves of any dirt
  3. Put the leaves in a pot with boiling water for 15 seconds and then drain all the water out of the leaves with a strainer
  4. Put in the blender the drained basil leaves, pecans, the two kinds of cheeses, the olive oil, the salt and blend all the ingredients together
  5. Add the cream milk or milk if you want it more creamy
  6. Make some pasta
  7. you can fry the garlic once you cut them in small pieces with some butter and then pour the pesto sauce together with the pasta
  8. Voila (there you have it...enjoy)
